2. Giftun Island: A Quiet and Beautiful Island

For a peaceful escape, just a short boat ride from the bustling crowds, Giftun Island offers an idyllic retreat. As you approach by boat, you're welcomed by stunning views of soft, white sandy beaches embraced by crystal-clear azure waters. There are no vehicles or buildings to disrupt the untouched natural beauty. On Giftun Island, you can unwind by sunbathing, snorkel in vibrant coral reefs, or simply float in the serene waters. Be sure to add Giftun Island to your must-see list when visiting Hurghada.

Address: Hurghada, Red Sea Governorate, Egypt

Timings: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Nearby Hotels: Available on mainland Hurghada.

Nearby Places to Visit: Mahmya Island, Orange Bay, Paradise Island Hurghada, Eden Island Hurghada
