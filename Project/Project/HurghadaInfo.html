Fascinating Past

Over the years, Hurghada has transformed into a major tourist destination along the Red Sea.

Why Hurghada Captivates Visitors

With a rich history dating back to ancient times, Hurghada was once a small fishing village. Today, it has evolved into a top tourist destination, attracting visitors from around the globe.

Exploring the History of Hurghada

Hurghada, founded in the early 20th century as a small fishing village, has developed into a major Red Sea resort since the 1980s, thanks to Egyptian and foreign investment. The city is known for its watersports, such as windsurfing, kitesurfing, yachting, scuba diving, and snorkeling.
